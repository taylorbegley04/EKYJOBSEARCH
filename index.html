Jobseekers are able to use EKYJOBSEARCH free of charge. They can complete an online resume through our Jobseekers page. You don't have to have complete this form, but it allows us to fit you to a position better. Using the search bar, you can search open jobs in a specific area; you can also search for a certain job, and it will tell you the areas that have that position open.

Companies have the option of paying $10 per job post, or they can create a membership that offers unlimited job postings for only $25 a month. When posting an opening, companies have the option to put the requirements needed for that position. Companies can also use the search bar to look for resumes.
